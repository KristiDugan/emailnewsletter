Dear Colleagues,

Welcome to the long-awaited second issue of the Shakespeare and Dance Dispatch, a news bulletin brought to you by the Shakespeare and Dance Project.

We are expanding the website and are seeking short articles and background essays, as well as additions and corrections to current content. We would also be delighted to highlight your Shakespeare and dance news in the next dispatch.

To contribute to the website, share your news, or offer your suggestions, please contact us at dancingshakespeare@gmail.com

Kind regards,

Emily Winerock, Amy Rodgers, and Linda McJannet
The Shakespeare and Dance Project
